Project Description: Music Recommendation Based on Facial Expressions
As a part of my research project, I spearheaded the development of an innovative Music Recommendation System that utilizes facial expressions to curate personalized playlists. The aim of the project was to explore the connection between human emotions and music preferences, leveraging computer vision techniques and machine learning algorithms. We collected a vast dataset of facial expressions captured through facial recognition technology while participants listened to a wide range of music genres. This dataset served as the foundation for training our machine learning models. Using deep learning algorithms and feature extraction techniques, I developed a robust facial expression recognition model capable of accurately detecting emotions such as happiness, sadness, excitement, and more.
